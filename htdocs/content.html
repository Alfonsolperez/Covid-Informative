The Virus

COVID-19

The COVID-19 is a contagious disease caused by a severe acute respiratory syndrome, the coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2). In December 2019, the first known case was discovered in Wuhan, China. Since then, the disease has spread worldwide, resulting in a major global pandemic.

Take Steps To Protect Yourself

Avoid Close Contact

When going outside, it is imperative that you try to avoid close contact with the people surrounding you at all times, so that you are able to decrease, and maybe even prevent the spread of the virus.

Wash Your Hands

When coming back home or when coming back from the outside, it is important that you wash your hands and keep yourself clean before doing anything else to ensure that you are cleansed of the germs from outside.

Wear a Face Mask

If going outside is necessary, it is very important that you wear a mask and other necessary safety equipment to keep you safe from the virus by preventing any entry of pathogens through the airway.

Symptoms to Look Out After

The Common and Less Common

Symptoms of COVID-19

Common Symptoms include: fever, cough, tiredness, loss of taste or smell. Less Common Symptoms include: sore throat, headaches, aches and pains, diarrhea, and rashes on the skin and discoloration of fingers or toes.

Next
The Serious

Symptoms of COVID-19

The Serious Symptoms include: difficulty in breathing, shortness of breath, loss of speech, loss of mobility, confusion, and chest pains. Seek immediate medical attention if you have serious symptoms.
