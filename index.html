Coronavirus

What are Coronaviruses?

Coronaviruses (CoV) are a broad group of viruses that can cause everything from a typical cold to more serious illnesses. A novel coronavirus (nCoV) is a new strain of coronavirus that has never been seen in humans before.

COVID-19

What is the COVID-19

COVID-19 is an illness caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. COVID-19 causes modest symptoms in the majority of people, but it can cause serious illness in some. Although the majority of patients with COVID-19 recover within weeks after becoming unwell, some people develop post-COVID symptoms.

Effects

The Effects of COVID-19 on our Society

The COVID-19 pandemic has resulted in a significant loss of human life around the world, and it poses an unprecedented threat to public health, food systems, and the workplace. The pandemic has wreaked havoc on the economy and society, putting tens of millions of people at risk of falling into abject poverty.
